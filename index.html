About

Based in Hangzhou, China, Xu Luyao is a media arts student at Hangzhou Normal University specialising in Broadcasting & Television Direction. She moves fluently between the roles of filmmaker, short video producer, and cultural communicator — turning observation into story, and story into impact.

Her work spans documentary journalism, brand content, and cultural events. Whether editing 10+ videos daily for a municipal media centre or producing a debut brand film that earned over ¥5,000 in its first five days, she brings craft and commercial instinct in equal measure.
